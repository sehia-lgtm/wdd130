Ivory Coast

Ivory Coast is a country located in West Africa. It is bordered by Liberia, Guinea, Mali, Burkina Faso, and Ghana. The country has a population of over 26 million people. The official language is French. The country is known for its cocoa production and is the world's largest producer of cocoa. The country is also known for its music and dance. The country has a rich culture and history. The country, brilliantly hosted and won the last African Nations Cup.
